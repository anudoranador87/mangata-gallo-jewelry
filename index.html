Our Story: From Austin, With Love The Journey of Mangata & Gallo

Founded by master jeweler Mariana, Mangata & Gallo was born from a passion for diamond cutting and traditional metal forging. After years of refining her craft, Mariana returned to her roots in Austin to open an atelier that blends classical elegance with Texas soul. Today, we continue to transform raw materials into exquisite legacies, keeping the artisanal spirit alive in every weld and setting.
